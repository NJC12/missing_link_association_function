Running a GWAS using genomes, phenotypes, and covariates from the UK Biobank.
- Coding SNPs used as covariates are selected in cojo/download_cummings_2019_data.sh and cojo/identifying_coding_variants.R
- Plink can be found at https://www.cog-genomics.org/plink/2.0/
- Bcftools at https://samtools.github.io/bcftools/bcftools.html
- UCSC LiftOver at https://genome.ucsc.edu/cgi-bin/hgLiftOver?hgsid=1215574455_tnqrIDUa1Wcml6e1TnvYHaQvNc1J
